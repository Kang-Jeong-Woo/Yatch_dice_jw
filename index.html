Yatch Dice

1. Start the Game: Press the SPACE to roll all five dice.

2. Select Dice: Double-click on dice to select or deselect them. Selected dice will move to a designated area.

3. Score Points: After selecting dice, Double-click the scoreboard for valid scoring options. Points will be automatically calculated and updated.

4. End the Round: After all five dice are selected and scored, press SPACE to roll again.

5. Win the Game: Keep rolling and scoring until all categories on the scoreboard are filled. The player with the highest score wins!
